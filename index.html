About Me

The straight-talking CS leader
you actually need.

Ten years in B2B SaaS has taught me that customers don't need you to tell them what they want to hear. They need to trust that what you're telling them is true. That's what I've built my career on.

That means having the conversations others sidestep. Setting realistic expectations. Telling a customer something isn't right for them, even when it costs a deal. It's not always comfortable, but it's what builds the kind of trust that keeps customers around and growing.

I can hold my own in a C-suite conversation and I'm just as happy getting into the detail of a broken onboarding process. I've built teams from scratch, redesigned customer lifecycle frameworks, and turned reactive CS functions into structured organisations people are proud to work in.

Based in Newcastle, working remotely or hybrid across the UK and further afield. I'm always interested in conversations with B2B SaaS businesses where doing right by the customer and doing right by the business are treated as the same thing.

"The best CS leaders aren't the ones who tell customers yes. They're the ones customers trust enough to hear no from."
I've built my career on commercial honesty, the kind that's actually in everyone's interest. Customers who are set up to succeed stay and grow. Customers who are oversold churn, and take the relationship with them. It really is that simple.
